ESTABLISHING PEACE AND ORDER

Welcome to the Saint Louis University Peace and Order advocacy web site promoted by the Office of Student Affairs.

We envision transformation, development, and discipline of students.

The Office of Student Affairs serves as an instrument of transformation in its task of creating an enhancing environment that fosters the holistic development of more than 30,000 students within one of the most prestigious Universities in the northern region of the Philippines.

Who We Are

The Office of Student Affairs serves as the instrument in the holistic development and transformation of students within the university. With the assistance of the Enriquez Security Staff, we are able to enforce and assure that peace and order is always observed in the campus at all times.

What We Do

We make sure that discipline and respect, not only to the rule of law but to others as well is upheld by the students and educators. With our God given knowledge and wisdom, we set an example of Excellence, Responsibility, and Christian Spirit to all students of this university.

Why We Do It

The vision of Saint Louis University was to be an excellent and transformative educational institution driven by the CICM missionary goals of service, and the tradition of Academic Excellence, Social Responsibiliy, and Christian Living.
